SNAKE GAME
About the game
Released in 1972, Pong is a table tennis-themed video game. Founded by Atari co-founder Nolan Bushnell, he conceived of the idea and assigned an engineer, Allan Alcorn, to create it.
Objective
Simple. The first to reach 10 points win! :D
Points
You gain a point for every time the pong ball touches your opponent’s side of the area. Nothing complicated.
Movement
Movement Press ‘W’ and ‘S’, Up and Down arrow key to move up and down respectively. The first pair is for P1 while the second is for P2.
