SNAKE GAME
About the game
Although it is not as old as the other games present on this website, snake game has also gained substantial popularity over the years, with Google’s remake of it being one of the most popular ones now. A timeless classic that only involves a snake and, what we think is, an apple.
Objective
Simple. Eat as many apples as possible and avoid hitting either yourself or the walls.
Points
Points are gained every time an apple is eaten. At the same time, your snake body also increases in length by one unit. The more you eat, the longer you are!
Movement
Press ‘W’, ‘A’, ‘S’ and ‘D’ to move up, left, down and right respectively. This works with the arrow keys as well.
